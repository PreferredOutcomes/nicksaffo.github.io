About Me



My name is Nick Saffo I am a senior at UNCW. I am majoring in Information Technology with a minor in Cyber Security. I wasnt sure what specific job I was looking for when I started working towards this major but I do know that I enjoy working with others to real a goal. I like being creative and designing things as well. I really enjoyed MIS-310 and 316 because they allowed me to get creative with the HTML, CSS, Java and C# to create interesting websites.

A couple hobbies of mine are tennis and golf. I started playing golf a year or so ago and at first did'nt think I would like it but it quickly became one of my favorite ways to spend free time. I am also a big starwars geek, hints the picture. I have one brother and a sister. My brother lives in New York city and is persuing a music career. My sister graduated ECU with a double major in psychology and criminal justice.

I really enjoy traveling, I have visited many places over the last few years. I really like Europe, I visited 8 countries there and hope to one day get the chance to live there for a few years at least.
